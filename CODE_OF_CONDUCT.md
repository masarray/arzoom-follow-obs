# Community Code of Conduct

ArZoom welcomes users and contributors from different backgrounds, experience levels, languages, and technical communities.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence and reproducible details.
- Keep discussions relevant to ArZoom, OBS workflows, documentation, testing, and development.
- Protect private, confidential, and customer information.
- Help beginners understand the next practical step.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing another person's private information
- Deliberately disruptive, inflammatory, or repetitive behavior
- Spam, malware, deceptive downloads, or unsafe instructions
- Sharing credentials, stream keys, confidential screens, or proprietary customer data

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

Report conduct concerns privately to `ari.sulistiono@gmail.com`. Include relevant links or screenshots, but avoid forwarding unnecessary personal or confidential information.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other project-managed spaces.
